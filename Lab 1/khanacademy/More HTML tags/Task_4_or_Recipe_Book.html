Recipe of Borscht

  • Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredient Quantity
Beets 2 pcs
Potatoes 3 pcs
White cabbage 300 g
Carrot 1 pc
Onion 1 pc
Tomato paste 2 tbsp
Meat with bone 500 g
Water 2 L
Garlic 2 cloves
Vegetable oil 2 tbsp
Salt, pepper, bay leaf to taste
Sour cream for serving

Step 1: Boil the meat Boil the meat with bone in 2 liters of water until tender. Strain the broth.
Step 2: Sauté the beets Cut the beets into thin strips and sauté in vegetable oil with tomato paste for 5-7 minutes.
Step 3: Add the potatoes Dice the potatoes and add them to the boiling broth. Cook for 10 minutes.
Step 4: Add the cabbage Add shredded cabbage to the broth and cook for another 5 minutes.
Step 5: Sauté the onion and carrot Sauté the onion and carrot, then add to the pot. Follow with the sautéed beets.
Step 6: Season the borscht Season the borscht with salt, pepper, bay leaf, and minced garlic. Simmer for 5 more minutes.
Step 7: Serve Serve with sour cream and fresh herbs.

Recipe of Pancakes

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredient Quantity
All-purpose flour 200 g
Milk 300 ml
Eggs 2 pcs
Sugar 2 tbsp
Butter (melted) 2 tbsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Vegetable oil for frying

Step 1: Mix dry ingredients In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt.
Step 2: Add milk Gradually add milk while whisking to avoid lumps.
Step 3: Add eggs Beat the eggs and add them to the batter. Stir well.
Step 4: Add butter Add melted butter and mix until smooth. Let the batter rest for 15 minutes.
Step 5: Heat the pan Heat a frying pan with a small amount of oil.
Step 6: Cook pancakes Pour a ladle of batter, swirling to cover the pan evenly. Cook each pancake for 1-2 minutes on each side until golden.
Step 7: Serve Serve with your favorite toppings like jam, honey, or syrup.

Recipe of Caesar Salad

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2-4
Ingredient Quantity
Romaine lettuce 1 head
Chicken breast 1 pc
Parmesan cheese 50 g
Croutons 1 cup
Caesar dressing 3 tbsp
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Salt, pepper to taste

Step 1: Prepare the chicken Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper, then pan-fry in olive oil until golden and cooked through. Slice into strips.
Step 2: Chop the lettuce Wash and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
Step 3: Combine ingredients In a large bowl, combine lettuce, croutons, sliced chicken, and Caesar dressing. Toss gently to coat.
Step 4: Add cheese Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
Step 5: Serve Serve immediately.
